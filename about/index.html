Hi, I’m Tim! I like to keep my identity small, but roughly speaking:

  • I often work as a software developer,
  • I love trying new hobbies and pursuits,
  • My curiousity exceeds my free time,
  • I’m Australian-born, (currently) Singapore-based.

timpieces is my online personal site, and a weakly-associated personal handle (finding my real name on the internet is not intended to be hard). The site is primarily for myself. I like trying new things, but I’m also very forgetful. Writing it down somewhere allows me to look back in a few years time and reminisce.

If you’re at-all interested in what’s on this site, please reach out! See contact info to get in touch.
