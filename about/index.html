timpieces is my online personal site, and a weakly-associated personal handle (finding my real name on the internet is not intended to be hard). This site serves as a blog, a journal of my endeavours, and will likely be a general catch-all for things I want to share.

Endeavours

For a long time I had been toying with the idea of some sort of ‘portfolio for things that add meaning to my life’. It’s very common to document your professional achievements, but rarely is there any emphasis on personal growth and exploring the world. I’ve called this ’endeavours’ as a catch-all term for hobbies, projects, and anything that I personally wanted to try. If you know of a more standard word for this, please let me know!

My main requirement for this was that the content had to live a long time. I’m expecting to build this up over the course of my life, and I don’t want some flavour-of-the-month web framework making it hard to maintain this. All of these endeavours are just stored and document in markdown files, and Hugo will kindly serve them with some tags based on their frontmatter.
