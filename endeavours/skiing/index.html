01 Jan 2024

I tried skiing for 3 days in early 2024.

Normally this would definitely not be enough to write about.

The only reason that I include it as an endeavour is that I’m very proud of having given it a shot.

I think skiing is very difficult to learn as an adult, especially without dedicated lessons or a lot of time to spend on it. I joined some friends for 3 days in Japan and tried skiing with no instruction at all, and my friends were snowboarders. It went as poorly as you’d expect.

The main reason I’m unlikely to try again is how dangerous skiing feels. I haven’t looked up the statistics (and probably I should), but the handwavy “Nah I’ve still ok” response I get when I mention it to skiiers and snowboarders doesn’t fill me with confidence.

Very happy to have tried. Will be very happy to never do again.
