01 Oct 2025

A friend and I got together over beers a few months ago (as of this writing in late 2025). We were brainstorming ideas to replace our friends who were leaving Singapore. We resolved to organise a photowalk to meet people and for my friend to improve his photography skills. The hook of our walk would be that it is followed by a social hour where the attendees share a beer.

We set up the group and started walking. We went from three attendees (just us) to around twenty attendees in a few weeks.

I was able to learn many new marketing skills from this:

  • I built an SEO-friendly website with claude/codex at https://shotsnshots.org
  • I learned how to use instagram for running a group. Creating posts and stories, using the inbuilt editor, etc.
  • We’ve used a number of event sites for marketing, and it’s been useful to know which sites are good for certain things.

I’ve never had to do something like this before, so it’s been very valuable knowledge. It feels likely I’ll be able to apply it in future.

Because it’s been such a public project, it’s been fun to discuss with friends and work on it together.
