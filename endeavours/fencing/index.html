01 Feb 2024

In Singapore I live in a building which contains my gym. On the way to the gym I noticed a fencing club, so I decided to give it a shot. I messaged the number and after a few months they said they were organising an adult beginners course. I signed up.

The class was foil. I diligently did my drills and learned my footwork. I did my parries and ripostes. We lunged… a lot.

After a few lessons we did some sparring and it became a lot more fun.

In the end I stopped because my fencing gym moved out of my building and to another part of Singapore. It would have gone from a 2 minute commute to a 30 minute commute, and I couldn’t justify that. One part of fencing that annoyed me is that it is too hard to get practice outside of class. I didn’t have a practice partner. The club itself mostly focuses on kids and the adults class doesn’t have much focus.
