01 Jan 2010

I played squash in my late teens, but just casually with my brother. Normally I wouldn’t bother considering this as an endeavour worth mentioning, except that it’s a sport that I’ve since had occasion to play casually quite a few times. I played with a friend during university, and colleagues at multiple workplaces. I’ve never had any formal training, but I’ve got enough of the basics to play a decent game.

The sport itself is well designed and good fun. I always preferred it to tennis. It felt more intense and there was much more playtime during a session. It was good reinforcement of hand-eye coordination that I’ve used in multiple other sports since (badminton, table tennis, tennis).

I can see myself playing more squash in the future.
