01 Jan 2016

I had two major phases of playing basketball:

  • For a year in primary school I played in a local league for a year,
  • During university I frequently played casual games with friends.

The time in my primary school team went very similar to my time playing AFL. Kids eh? Not much to say here but I remember being terrible in games, but really enjoying practice.

University gave me plenty of spare time, so I took to improving at shooting hoops. The games were much more casual, half-court, and often we’d just do shooting games. I got a lot of enjoyment out of this, and later in life when living near a basketball court I usually buy a basketball so that I can shoot hoops. I think my form is decent and I hope to live near a hoop again sometime soon.
