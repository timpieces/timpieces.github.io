01 May 2024

I chose yoga because it was so different to any hobbies that I had tried before. Also, it was near my apartment. Yoga Movement is a very commercial yoga chain in Singapore. I’m a big fan of what they’ve done to commoditize yoga, as I’m not sold on the spiritual side of yoga. I downloaded their app and booked some classes. I did the basic class many many times.

Without trying to sound bitter, yoga seems like an activity designed mostly for women’s bodies. They seem to have a much better level of default flexibility, which makes it much more enjoyable. It’s achievable for men (evidenced by the male yogis at the studio), but it seems to take much more effort.

I’m quite competitive and didn’t like that my classmates weren’t taking the class very seriously. I saw very few shaking legs during warrior 1, and I felt like I was the only one trying to push myself.

In my experience there are two types of people who like yoga:

  • those who like the stretching,
  • those who like the mindfulness.

I found myself disliking both of these. The stretching wasn’t useful when only performed once per week. The mindfulness felt unmotivated and fake. It didn’t make me skeptical of yoga as a whole, but it made me realize that I wouldn’t enjoy continuing.
