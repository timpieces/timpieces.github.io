The world is a big place. I like trying new things; too many things to keep them all in my brain.

Below is a collection of many things I have tried. Some endeavours are multi-year hobbies, some (few) are weekend whims. The only common thread is that I’m proud of having tried them all.

Filter Tips

Use the filters to slice by type, tag, or the four scorecards (effort, learning, enjoyment, and success). Longer-form reflections live on each endeavour’s page. The scorecards are:

  • Effort: How much time and energy I put into the endeavour (1-5)
  • Learning: How much I learned from the endeavour (1-5)
  • Enjoyment: How much I enjoyed the endeavour (1-5)
  • Success: How successful I felt the endeavour was (1-5)
