bouldering

Created Oct 1, 2021 - Last updated: Oct 1, 2021

sport enjoyed-yes success-no

I started bouldering with some friends and colleagues in Sydney towards the end of 2021. Some of them were strong boulderers and they were all great people.

I learned some basic technique from youtube videos, and had an initial period of improving quickly. Still, I never bouldered enough to get past the beginner level.

I hated ripping skin off my fingers (as a full time software developer), everything else about the sport is quite enjoyable. The waiting around in bouldering is a good way to relax and take things slowly.

Bouldering/climbing is a sport that I’ve done socially much more than I’d have expected, so it’s been useful to have some basic technique in it.
