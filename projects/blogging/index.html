I started blogging in 2024 with my partner. I’d been wanting to try it out for some time, and so I encouraged us to try to blog frequently to get over our shared fear of public writing. It worked well. We blogged every two days for a few months.

We dropped it and picked it up again over the next year(s).

Come late 2025 and I have more time on my hands, I decide to supplement the frequent low-quality blogging with timpieces.me . I thought it would be nice to have a place to put more substantial writing, usually of a technical nature. At least that’s the plan for now. If I find that I’m not writing enough, I’ll likely aim to publish lower quality more frequent snippets, as the practice of writing brings the major benefit for me.
